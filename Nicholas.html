Alexander "Xander" Harris

Nicholas Brendon plays the funny and most times wimpy (unless it has something to do with Buffy) Xander. Xander is the king of sarcasm. You can find his infamous quotes (called Xanderisms) at almost any Buffy site.

Latest favourite quote: "A slayer, huh? I knew this 'I'm the only one' thing was just an attention-getter!" (Xander/Kendra) from the episode "What's my Line - part 2", Nov. 24, 1997.

It finally happened! Xander and Cordy collapsed in each others arms in the episode "What's my Line - part 2". If these two get together they are going to need relationship counselling after their first date! Can't wait to see what unfolds for these two in the near future.
