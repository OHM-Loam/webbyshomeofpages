Buffy Summers

Sarah Michelle Gellar plays the lead character Buffy Summers. The story goes something like this: For every generation there is a chosen one. The chosen one is capable of fighting and destroying anything that resembles evil (vampires, ghosts, and other assorted demons).

Therefore, Buffy is "the slayer", a title it seems she would rather not have. Buffy has enough problems as it is, riddled with your typical teen problems of popularity, boyfriends and grades. However, when the fit hits the shan Buffy is there to save the world and keep the formidable "Hell Mouth" from totally wrecking her day.
