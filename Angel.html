ANGEL

David Boreanaz plays the character Angel, a 240 year old vampire. About a century ago Angel fed on a Romani maiden. In retaliation, her clan placed a curse on Angel and he regained his soul while remaining trapped in vampire form.

He is in love with Buffy (yes, the vampire slayer!) and this only makes things worse. To quote the theme of the show: For every generation, there is a chosen one... but in Angel's case, he's just trying to survive his own past.
