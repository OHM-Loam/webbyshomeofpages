Cordelia

Charisma Carpenter plays the snobby, materialistic character of Cordelia Chase. For the second season, Cordelia has finally found out that Buffy is a slayer. Cordelia wants to help, however if it means breaking a nail, then forget it.

Besides, she is too busy bickering with Xander. These two need each other so bad it's not even funny! It finally happened in the episode "What's my Line - Part 2" on Nov. 24. I roared when they suddenly jumped into each others arms passionately kissing. We will have to wait and see the direction their relationship goes in the coming episodes. I always thought they would make a good couple. E-me with your thoughts.
