Rupert Giles

Anthony Stewart Head plays Rupert Giles, the "watcher" and school librarian. The watcher is responsible for training the slayer both mentally and physically.

You can always find Giles with his head buried into a book older than most vampires. Recognize Anthony? He's the guy from the Taster's Choice coffee commercials!
