Willow Rosenberg

Alyson Hannigan plays Willow Rosenberg, Buffy's best friend and slayerette. Willow is a computer nut that can hack her way into any top secret file in the nation.

Her seemingly never ending crush on Xander has finally subsided and she finally met Oz, a fellow classmate and lead guitarist in a local band. Oz, played by Seth Green, has had his eye on her since the start of the second season. We need an episode focused on Willow!
