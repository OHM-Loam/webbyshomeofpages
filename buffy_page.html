Hello, and welcome to my page devoted to my favorite T.V. show, Buffy The Vampire Slayer. To sum it up, BTVS is about a teenager that carries the weight of the world's safety on her shoulders as well as a teenager's everyday problems. Buffy has more trouble making sure she has the "hairstyle of the month" rather than preventing the formidable Hellmouth from engulfing the world!
